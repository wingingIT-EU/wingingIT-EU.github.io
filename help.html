Rules

Game goals

This is a game for 3 or more players. Each player except the spies gets a card with a word, which is the same for everyone. The spies get a special card. Nobody knows who are the spies and who are the common folk.

Spy's goal:

To stay undetected, until the time expires, or to guess the word

Other players' goal:

To guess who the spy or spies are.

If one of the players thinks that he/she has detected a spy, participants start voting. If all players who are not spies vote for one person, that person is out. In this case, he/she tries to guess he word. If they guesses correctly and they are the spy, that spy/spies win the game. Otherwise the common folk win. If the player is not a spy, or if there are more spies, the game continues and the person voted out must stay silent.

How to play

1. Chose your settings: number of players, number of spies and dictionary

2. Press play

3. Press 'View Card' to view your card

4. Press 'Hide Card' to hide your card and pass the phone/computer to the person beside you

5. Repeat 3 and 4 until all players have seen their card

6. Set a timer ( Recommended: between 5 and 15 minutes or 2 minutes multiplied by the number of players )

7. If the timer has expired, players must have one last vote and eliminate one person.

8. If there are any spies still playing, the spies win, but if the common folk have managed to vote out all the spies, the common folk win.

Have fun!
